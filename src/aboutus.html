About us

Kristine Gonchar, President and CEO of Elite IT Group, brings over 18 years of technology engineering and business experience to her leadership of the company. Kristine has extensive experience leading teams in enterprise-level projects for such companies as CVS/Caremark, AON Corporation and Allstate Corporation. She holds a Master of Science in Business from Latvia’s preeminent university, Latvian University, and leverages substantial experience in managing local and multinational teams in thesuccessful execution of projects. She is a noted expert in software quality assurance and Agile software development methodologies, holds respected industry certifications, and brings a hands-on approach to ensuring the success of Elite IT Group’s projects.

She is also an active participant in the Woman Owned Business community.
